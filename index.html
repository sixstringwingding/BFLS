About Us

Denny and Sheila Lamson were born just across the California border in Siskiyou County. High school sweethearts, they recently celebrated their 46th wedding anniversary. They have three adult children, 9 grandchildren and most recently, their first great grandchild.

​

Denny and Sheila moved to Jackson County, Oregon in 1986, where Denny began a 30-year career in financial services. For the final chapter of their work life, Denny and Sheila recently purchased the Southern Oregon and Central Oregon Tabasco Brands Specialty Foods and Gifts distributorship. Their goal is to bring these products along with many other fine food and gift lines from some the best companies they can find. Staying active and working together while enjoying friendly people, fun events, and great food, seems to them to be the perfect final chapter of what has been a wonderful life. They believe they have truly saved the “Best For Last”.
